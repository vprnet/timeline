Timeline is a project from VPR Classical, a service of Vermont Public Radio.

It is intended to be an informative, entertaining overview of music history. The format and content of Timeline comes from the research and study of the author and is not meant to be comprehensive or the last word on the subject. This work will hopefully stir the curious on to study music history further. Please see the suggested reading page on our website for references and suggestions of how to go deeper.

The podcast is made possible, in part, by a grant from the Ida and William Rosenthal Foundation.
